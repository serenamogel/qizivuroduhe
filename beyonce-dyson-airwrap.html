Beyonc's New Post *Finally* Convinced Us To Purchase A Dyson Airwrap

July 03, 2024
blog

If there is one thing we're jealous of in life, it’s Beyoncés hair (and singing abilities, of course). But we can only do something about one of those, thanks to Queen Bey’s latest Instagram post. The Texas Hold Em’ singer recently dropped the secret to her sleek blowouts and hair wash ritual. Beyoncé wrote, “Being disruptive and challenging everything people feel should be the process has always been exciting to me. My hair and music seemed to do that a lot over the years…” The Partition singer has maintained blonde on her natural hair for 25 years, which has become a pretty iconic look to the BeyHive (AKA fans of Beyoncé).

The pop star is videoed using the Dyson Supersonic hair dryer with the wide-tooth comb attachment to stretch out her curls. For curly-haired people, wide-tooth combs are usually the way to go to avoid breakage. This hair dryer costs $429 and comes with five different attachments.

Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer

It's currently available for purchase on Amazon in the colors nickel/copper or pink and rose gold (for reference, Bey used the nickel/copper one!). The hair tool is advertised to prevent extreme heat damage and protect hair’s natural shine.

To style her hair to perfection, Beyoncé and her team opted for the Dyson Airwrap multi-styler, consisting of five attachments. The real ones will remember when this hair appliance totally blew up on social media, with many users swearing by the tool. Again, it’s out of stock on Dyson’s website (once a viral product, always a viral product!), but you can snag one on Amazon for $599.

Dyson Airwrap

Beyoncé recently broke streaming records with her studio album, Cowboy Carter, which became the most streamed album in a single day in 2024. It also topped the Billboard 200. We are totally ready to style our hair while singing a Beyoncé album.
