Sarkodies Sisters Give Their Mother A Special Treat As She Celebrates Her 64th Birthday

May 28, 2024
blog

Madam Emma Ewurama Maame Aggrey who happens to be the mother of Ghana’s favorite rapper, Michael Owusu Addo, also known as Sarkodie recently celebrated her 64th birthday.

The rapper along with his sisters, Veronica Owusu Addo and Hannah Owusu Addo decided to give their beloved mother a special treat ass she turns a year older.

Unlike last year when the rapper and his siblings threw a plush birthday party for their mother, this year’s version was on a lowkey as they took her to a location to spend some quality time with her.

It will be recalled that pictures and videos of Maame Aggrey surfaced online especially on Twitter on her 63rd birthday.

In a video sighted on social media, Mama Sark was seen jamming to one of her favorite tunes while being driven by one of her children.

Meanwhile, the minster of Trade and Industry, Alan Jon Kyeremanten has paid the National Chief Imam, Osmanu Nuhu Sharubutu a visit at his residence.

Upon his visit, the Minister received the blessing of the National Chief Imam as he asks for the blessings of Allah upon him in all his endeavors.

He also used the occasion to donate some food items to the National Chief Imam and his household as they go through the month of Ramadan.
