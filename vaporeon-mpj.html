Vaporeon (Japanese: シャワーズ Showers) is a Pokémon who appeared in Magical Pokémon Journey.

History

Vaporeon debuts in The Master Plan... for Romance! where a Machamp falls in love with her. She also loves Machamp, but doesn't know that he loves her. Vaporeon later finds out about Machamp's feelings in Loves Me? Loves Me Not? and they go out on a date together.
