Early Life and Career Beginnings

Sally Jessy Raphael was born on February 25, 1935, in Easton, Pennsylvania. She began her career in broadcasting in Puerto Rico, where she hosted a morning show. Her career took off when she moved to Miami and started working in radio, eventually transitioning to television.
