Cranberries are one of my favorite seasonal ingredients. I’ve gathered together Crave Worthy Cranberry Recipes here for you to choose from. Enjoy one (or more!) of these delicious cranberry recipes with your family this holiday season!

Cranberry Recipes

I love enjoying cranberries during the holiday season. It just seems to be THE flavor that I am craving the most. I think the tartness of the cranberries is what makes them so special. Their bright flavor pairs so wonderfully with so many other flavors and goes great in both savor and sweet applications.

These recipes are the the cranberry recipes that I have shared with you over the  years and remain some of my favorite recipes to this day. Sweet desserts, savory and sweet rolls, and even a cocktail! There is definitely something here for everyone!

White Chocolate Cranberry Cheesecake – This is the the single most beautiful dessert I have ever created. I love the elegance of the sugared cranberries and the decadence of the white chocolate ganache topping. It’s truly a showstopper!

5 SECRETS TO GETTING DINNER ON THE TABLE...FAST!

This extra delicious and supremely moist Cranberry Pecan Pumpkin Bread is bursting with fall flavors! So easy to make to and a guaranteed hit with friends and family this holiday season.

Look no further for the Best Ever Cranberry Sauce! This easy and delightful recipe takes only 15 minutes to make and a handful of ingredients! Spiced with cinnamon and sweetened with orange juice, it is the best combination of sweet and tart!

Don’t want to make your cranberry sauce on the stove? Try this Crockpot Cranberry Sauce instead! Spiced with cinnamon and sweetened with orange juice, it is the best combination of sweet and tart! This easy recipe takes just minutes of prep work and only a handful of ingredients!

These Cranberry Pecan Brie Bites are perfect for holiday entertaining! Whether you make them for Thanksgiving, Christmas, or New Year’s, no one will be able to resist the gooey melted brie, tart cranberry sauce, and toasted pecan atop a piece of naan! Easy and fabulous – just what holiday entertaining should be!

Cranberry Pecan Mini Goat Cheese Balls! Holiday entertaining has never been easier or more delicious! So easy to make and gorgeous too! Perfect for Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s! (Can be made in advance!)

An impressive yet delightfully simple appetizer that is perfect for any celebration – Slow Cooker Cranberry Orange Meatballs! Just five ingredients and tons of delicious flavor!

Cranberry Orange Shortbread Cookies – This recipe was an instant success. Everyone loves the ease of a shortbread cookie and the cranberry and orange combination is classically delicious. Tip: Don’t give up when your dough doesn’t seem to be coming together. There is ONE CUP of butter that needs to get worked in before the dough forms. It will happen!

Rolls are a must with any holiday meal and your guests will no doubt be pleased with these Cranberry Yeast Rolls. Made with pumpkin pie spice, they have a distinctly unique flavor that  no one can resist.

Got 3 minutes? Then you can make this Small Batch Cranberry Pistachio Fudge! Great for a smaller gathering or as a gift!

 I love utilizing my slow cookers for the holidays. They are just so darn convenient! One of my favorite recipes to make in my slow cooker is this Slow Cooker Spiced Cranberry Chutney. Guaranteed to make your home smell amazing!

It’s fun to serve up holiday cocktails at parties and this Cranberry Mojito is both easy and fun! Garnish with fresh cranberries, lime and mint for a festive presentation.

White Chocolate Cranberry Pistachio Shortbread is an easy and delicious treat that everyone loves. I like to cut it into bars so it’s easier to grab and go. Dipping in white chocolate is optional but adds a touch of elegance to these cookies.

One of the most popular recipes on my site during the holidays, this creamy White Chocolate Cranberry Fudge is my VERY favorite fudge recipe of all time.

I would happily put a cheesecake layer in ANYTHING. It goes particularly well in these sweet Oatmeal Cranberry Cheesecake Bars. This recipe is great for a crowd!

What every holiday table needs – Cranberry Fluff!

So pretty, so easy, these Cranberry Pecan Pinwheel Cookies are bound to make you smile!

This Slow Cooker Spiced Cranberry Citrus Apple Cider is the beverage of choice this season! Fill your home with it’s delightful fragrance and your belly with it’s spicy warmth! Perfect for parties and the holidays!

I sure hope you’ve found a cranberry recipe here that you can make and enjoy with your family this holiday season! Happy holidays!
