Talanoa Hufanga, who plays as safety for the San Francisco 49ers, has a statistical record of 43 tackles, 1 touchdown and 1 interception.

Talanoa Hufanga PFF

Hufanga’s record includes 14 solo tackles, 2 assists, 1 interception.

Talanoa Hufanga Contract

Hufanga signed a four-year deal with the San Francisco 49ers worth $3,480,000 and earns a base salary of $825,000.

Talanoa Hufanga Draft

Hufanga was drafted by the 49ers as 180th pick in the 5th round of the 2021 NFL draft.

Talanoa Hufanga Jersey

Hufanga wears jersey number 29 in the 49ers.

Talanoa Hufanga 40 Time

Hufanga runs a 4.61 40-yard dash.

Hufanga went to Crescent Valley High School in Corvallis, Oregon, where he played football as a quarterback, safety, and wide receiver.

He was named the Polynesian High School National Player of the Year in his senior year.

Hufanga participated in the 2018 U.S. Army All-American Game.

Hufanga continued on to play collegiate football at the University of Southern California (USC).

He made eight appearances, made five starts, and recorded 51 tackles in his rookie season.

Hufanga has 90 tackles and 3.5 sacks in his second season while playing in all 10 games and starting all of them.
