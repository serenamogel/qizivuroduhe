CHECK OUT THIS FIRST LOOK TEASER OF DISNEYS THE SECRETS OF SULPHUR SPRINGS SEASON 2

Disney’s The Secrets of Sulphur Springs is gearing up for another season and fans could not be happier. Check out this first look teaser of Disney’s The Secrets of Sulphur Springs season two.

First off, if you aren’t familiar with Disney’s The Secrets of Sulphur Springs, let’s give you a brief overview from the beginning.

Disney’s The Secrets of Sulphur Springs, follows 12-year-old Griffin Campbell and a thrill-seeking classmate, Harper, as they pair discovers a secret portal that allows them to travel back in time and uncover clues about a mystery involving a girl who disappeared decades ago from a Louisiana town.

So what is in store for fans this season? Well, check out this synopsis below.

In season two, Griffin Campbell, Harper Dunn and Savannah discover there are more mysteries to unravel about The Tremont, Harper’s deep-seeded roots and connection to the hotel, the origin of the portal and time traveling machine as well as the identity of the Tremont’s real ghost.

When Harper follows a ghost back to the 1930s, she meets some of her some relatives including her great uncle Sam (Hutchison), her great great grandfather Elijah (Manning Jr.) and her great great grandmother Grace (Thompson). In addition, Harper encounters her great grandmother Daisy, a role also played by Kyleigh.

In addition to the new season’s action and adventure, the series will also add five new cast members to the show! Johari Washington, Ethan Hutchison, Eugene Byrd, Kenneisha Thompson and Robert Manning Jr have all joined the cast of the popular show. These talented actors will star alongside returning cast mates, Preston Oliver (Griffin), Kyliegh Curran (Harper), Elle Graham (Savannah), Josh Braaten (Ben), Kelly Frye (Sarah), Landon Gordon (Wyatt), Madeleine McGraw (Zoey) and Diandra Lyle (Jess).

Season two of The Secrets of Sulphur Springs will premiere in January 2022 on Disney Channel and Disney+. However, if you want to catch up now before the new season begins, do so on Disney+. It is definitely worth the binge!
