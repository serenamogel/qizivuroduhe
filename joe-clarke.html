Joe Michael Clarke is a professional cricketer, a right-handed batsman, and a wicketkeeper. His brother’s name is Robert Clarke who is a domestic cricketer of England. He is known for being a player of Nottinghamshire County Cricket Club.

Career Journey

Joe Clarke first started as a player in first-class cricket. In 2015, he played his first match for team Worcestershire versus team Hampshire. After that, he played a T20 International match for team England against Pakistan in 2015-16 Pakistan A v England Lions unofficial T20I Series in which he gave his best performance as a batsman. In 2017, Joe again becomes part of team England Lions in Australia tour. In 2018, he played in Vitality Blast 2018 for the team Worcestershire. Later in 2019, Joe played in the 2019 Twenty20 Cup (England) for team Nottinghamshire, it was a big tournament of his career. In 2020, Joe played for the team Perth Scorchers in Big Bash League. Now, Joe Clarke is a team member of Karachi Kings in Pakistan Super League season 6 and he is giving his best performance in this league.

Achievements

Joe Michael Clarke is a very remarkable and talented cricketer of England. He has also given his best performance in every match that he played. In 2020, he won the title of Leading Run Scorer in Nottinghamshire. In the 2020 Big Bash League, he achieved an award of T20 Player of the Year. Joe is still doing his best for his team Karachi Kings in Pakistan Super League season 6.
